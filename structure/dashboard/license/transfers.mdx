## System Migrations

In the event that Leaflux Group migrates its licensing system to a new platform or service, all affected users will be notified and given a defined deadline to transfer their licenses.

<Danger>
  If you fail to transfer your licenses before the stated deadline, Leaflux Group is **not obligated** to grant, restore, or honor those licenses. No exceptions will be made after the deadline has passed.
</Danger>

<Info>
  As a courtesy, you may request a **late license migration** after a deadline has passed by paying a fee of **100 Robux** or **€2.00 EUR**, depending on your original payment method. This fee is non-refundable and late migration requests are subject to approval at Leaflux Group's discretion, they are not guaranteed.
</Info>

## Account-to-Account Transfers

Users may request a license transfer to a different account. All such requests are reviewed and approved or denied at the **sole discretion of Leaflux Group**.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When a transfer may be approved" icon="circle-check">
    - The license is registered to an active account in good standing
    - The receiving account has not violated the Leaflux Group Terms of Service
    - The request is submitted through the official support channel with sufficient detail
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When a transfer will not be permitted" icon="circle-xmark">
    - The originating account has been suspended, banned, or blacklisted
    - The product or asset is marked as non-transferable
    - The transfer would violate any provision of the Terms of Service
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  You are **fully responsible** for providing and maintaining access to the destination account. Leaflux Group is not liable for any loss of access, ownership disputes, or inability to recover a license if the receiving account is not under your control or becomes inaccessible.
</Warning>
