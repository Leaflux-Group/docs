## Stripe & PayPal Purchases

Refunds for purchases made via Stripe or PayPal are not guaranteed and are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When you may be eligible for a refund" icon="circle-check">
    - The refund request is submitted within a reasonable timeframe following the date of purchase
    - A verified issue exists with the functionality or accessibility of the purchased product or service
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When a refund will not be granted" icon="circle-xmark">
    - Your account has violated the Leaflux Group Terms of Service
    - The request is based on a change of mind or dissatisfaction following use of the product
    - A chargeback or payment dispute has already been initiated through your payment provider
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Info>
  To request a refund, please visit our [Contact Us](/contact-us) page and reach out through the appropriate support channel. When submitting your request, include your Roblox username, Discord ID, and a clear description of the issue.
</Info>

## Robux Purchases

All purchases made with Robux are considered **final and non-refundable** by default due to the nature of the Roblox platform.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When a refund may be considered" icon="circle-check">
    - A critical issue is identified that prevents access to or use of the purchased asset, and the issue is confirmed to originate from Leaflux Group
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When a refund will not be granted" icon="circle-xmark">
    - The purchase was completed successfully and the asset is accessible
    - The request is based on a change of mind or user error
    - Roblox platform limitations prevent the reversal of the transaction
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  Robux refunds are subject to Roblox platform constraints. Leaflux Group cannot guarantee the technical feasibility of any Robux refund regardless of eligibility.
</Warning>
