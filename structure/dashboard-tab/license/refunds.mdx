## Stripe/PayPal Purchases

Refunds for Stripe or PayPal purchases are not guaranteed.

### You may be eligible for a refund if:

- The request is made within a reasonable time after purchase
- There is a valid issue with functionality or access

### Refunds will not be granted if:

- You have violated the Terms of Service
- The request is based on change of mind or dissatisfaction after use
- A chargeback or dispute has already been initiated
